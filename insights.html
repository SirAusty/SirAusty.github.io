M01

Mechanical Market Structure & Order Flow

Retail traders view structure subjectively through trendlines and chart patterns. The algorithmic delivery engine operates strictly on mechanical structural point calculations. Order flow represents the systemic continuation or shifting of institutional capital frames.

Break of Structure (BOS)

Occurs when the market prints a clear, definitive candle body close in the direction of the dominant macro trend. Minor wick filter breaches do not constitute structural validity.

Change of Character (CHoCH)

The initial structural signal indicating an order flow reversal sequence. Validated only when the protected swing low/high that engineered the final trending swing point is fully broken by a candle body close.

Axiom: Protected vs. Targeted Elements In a bullish trend framework, swing lows are structurally protected by institutional buy orders. If a swing low is breached before a fresh structural high is printed, structural alignment collapses.
M02

Institutional Liquidity Pools & Sweeps

The interbank algorithm cannot deploy large-scale capital entries without matching buy or sell orders. Market spikes do not seek patterns; they actively seek clusters of capital exposure (stop losses) to engineer transactions.

The Judas Swing Execution Matrix

An intentional, deceptive price run designed to sweep structural liquidity engineering points right at session openings (e.g., London Open or New York Open). The algorithm drives prices violently past previous session parameters, forces retail break-out triggers into traps, captures the liquid flow, and then reverses cleanly back down the primary projection vector.

M03

Order Blocks & Fair Value Gaps (FVG)

Institutional execution footprints present themselves inside extreme volume changes. We measure these via displacement footprints on high-volatility parameters like the Volatility 75 Index (VIX).

Institutional Order Block (OB)

The final structural candle sequence printed right before an explosive structural breakdown or expansion occurs. This zone represents heavy residual institutional buy or sell limits that remain unmitigated. Price returns here to balance the books before continuing.

Fair Value Gap (FVG) / Imbalance

A specific 3-candle structural sequence marking highly inefficient price routing. Validated when Candle 1's wick and Candle 3's wick fail to touch, leaving an explicit structural window of pure single-sided delivery. The algorithm naturally repairs this void.

Mechanical Check: Mitigation Sequence Validation

An order block loses structural execution probability every time its 50% equilibrium threshold (Mean Threshold) is completely pierced by body closures. Isolate fresh, unmitigated footprints for absolute sniper executions.
