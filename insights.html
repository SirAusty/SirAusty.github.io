Master the mechanics of synthetic indices with zero guesswork.

An FVG occurs when a sudden burst of buying or selling leaves a gap in the price action. In synthetic indices like V75, price often returns to these zones to rebalance before continuing its original trend.
Markets actively seek liquidity. A sweep happens when price pushes past obvious retail highs or lows to trigger stop-losses before rapidly reversing. We wait for these sweeps to enter trades alongside institutional momentum.
An Order Block is the last opposite-closing candle before a strong move that breaks market structure. It represents smart money accumulation or distribution, acting as a high-probability zone for our entries.
