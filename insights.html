Module 1: Advanced Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

An Order Block (OB) is not just a regular "support or resistance" zone. It represents a specific price level where commercial trading algorithms have flooded the market with heavy institutional volume, creating a massive imbalance.

The Invalidation & Setup Protocol:

1. The Structural Shift: Price must cleanly break a recent swing high or low with strong momentum. This is your Market Structure Shift (MSS) or Change of Character (CHoCH).

2. Identifying the Block: Locate the final opposite candle right before that heavy displacement started.

3. Execution Engine: Set limit orders at the opening price of that candle, or at the 50% equilibrium level (Mean Threshold).

The 3-Candle Blueprint:
