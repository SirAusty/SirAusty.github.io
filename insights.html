Module 1: Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

An Order Block (OB) represents the last opposite candle before an institutional displacement of price that breaks structural points (BOS/CHoCH).

  • Bullish OB: The lowest down-close candle prior to a sharp structural break upward.
  • Bearish OB: The highest up-close candle prior to a sharp structural break downward.

We wait for price to return to the open or 50% equilibrium level of this block for precision entries, stacking orders with tight invalidation points directly beyond the block's tail.

A Fair Value Gap (FVG) is a 3-candle structural imbalance created by violent market inefficiency. It exists when the wick of Candle 1 and the wick of Candle 3 do not overlap, leaving a void in Candle 2.

Synthetic algorithms return to these imbalances to deliver fair pricing. Stacking entries inside an FVG confluence with an premium/discount zone offers high risk-to-reward ratios.

⚠️ REBEL RULE: Never chase a fast-moving candle. The algorithm is programmed to balance the FVG it leaves behind. Wait for the fill.
